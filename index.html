Who's it for?

Resonant is for anyone who learns better by listening. The student pulling an all-nighter. The commuter who'd rather listen than read. The curious mind that just prefers to hear.

And at its heart, it's built for the students who've always deserved better tools. For the blind and visually impaired learners who were handed a screen reader and told that was enough. It never is enough.
